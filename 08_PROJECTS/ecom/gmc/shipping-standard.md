# Стандартные параметры доставки для GMC (US Store)

Применять ко всем новым GMC если иное не указано.

## Shipping policy text

Processing Time:
Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Once your order is confirmed, we'll get it ready and out the door fast.

Estimated Delivery Time:
Shipping usually takes 3–10 business days, depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking link as soon as your order is on its way.

Total delivery: 1–2 business days processing + 3–10 business days transit (4–12 business days total).
