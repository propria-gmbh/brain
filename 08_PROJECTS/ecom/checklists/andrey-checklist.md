- [ ] No empty pages or content

  - comment: REDIRECT 404 ERRORS

## Not Allowed

- Don't duplicate themes across stores. If copied: change everything in edit code (e.g. previous store name)
- Don't use pictures of store name on a physical store
- Don't show shipping time on product page (lowers conversion rate)
- Don't create scarcity with "5 products left in stock"
- Don't show trust badges of established brands (Forbes, fake Trustpilot)
- Money Back Guarantee: replace with "14/30 day return period (per return policy)"
- No "100% Satisfaction"
- Use TM or © only if legally owned
- Avoid fake urgency (countdown timers, limited stock claims)
- No "High quality (material)"

## Top Reasons for GMC Rejection

- Payment methods not consistent across all pages
- Timezone mismatch between GMC and store
- Shipping/return policies don't match GMC settings
- Broken links or buttons without function
- Too many products with compared-at-price (>50%)
- Fake reviews or testimonials
- Urgency elements (timers, countdowns)
- Trust badges that aren't verified
- Company block missing on important pages
- Theme/product imports from other stores
