01 — Concept
"Linen for the unhurried life."

Casa Giannini is a branded DTC linen brand built on Mediterranean lifestyle aesthetics. Phase 1: branded dropshipping with no inventory risk. Phase 2: private label brand with own woven label.

Previous experience confirmed demand for linen goods. The niche is growing at the intersection of slow fashion, sustainability, and aspirational living. Target customer: women 25–45, Italy and US markets. Minimum order value: $100.
